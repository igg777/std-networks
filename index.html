Standards Network
This is a subset of the movie network produced from data in the mobile Standards for I4.0! app. Standards for I4.0 relate to each other as they get picked by Standards for I4.0! users
  • Hover over a node to see the Standard title, and to highlight the Standard related to it.
  • Click on a node to show Standard details. It will open up a side panel.
  • On the Standard details panel, you can click on the target icon to center the graph on that movie. And clicking on the link to a related Standard will move to that Standard (in the graph and in the panel)
  • You can use you usual browser controls to zoom and pan over the graph. If you zoom in when centred on a movie, the graph will expand around it.
  • Above a certain zoom level, movie titles are automatically displayed for all the nodes.
